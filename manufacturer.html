Features

Your Manufacturing transformation begins here.

Product Definition & Identification.

EDGE Pharma offers the flexibility of SKU management with the robust inbuilt granularity that your business needs to define and identify your products via product codes/barcode, product measures, volume and tonnage metrics.

Stock State Management.

Utilize the inbuilt auto Stock State Management Feature and take charge of fluctuations in your stock state with corresponding notifications.

Credit Management & Reconciliation.

EDGE Pharma’s credit management system monitors, and tracks credit transactions of Distributors based on credit limit segmentation and principles that have been laid out taking into consideration the acceptable risk.

Product Price Management.

In order to stay ahead of your competitors, your business needs a robust Price Management System. EDGE Pharma offers this by providing a flexible solution that supports changes to your benchmark pricing, pricing rules, regional product pricing, promotional pricing and exception pricing - allowing you to focus on what matters most to your business, maximizing those profits.
