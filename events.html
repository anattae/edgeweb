EDGE AT THE ANNUAL PSN CONFERENCE HELD AT IBADAN
The Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN) recently held its 91st Annual National Conference, with the theme “INNOVATIVE DISRUPTION IN PHARMACY AND EMERGING ECONOMIES. A ROAD MAP FOR NIGERIA”. Its highpoint was centered on enabling positive disruption within the Pharmaceutical Industry through Information Technology. The event which was full of glitz and glamour had in attendance top personalities and practitioners of the Pharmaceutical profession and showcased EDGE PHARMA, the disruptive technology solution for the pharma industry, running on the PharmiT platform and powered by NETOP. The interaction of the EDGE Pharma team with participants was a huge success as many could not contain their excitement as to how the solution would radically improve their businesses. The EDGE solution seamlessly connects all actors in the Pharmaceutical value chain via a single platform. It offers business process control, real-time global inventory, visibility, business intelligence/dashboards, comprehensive product management and online access to customizable reports, bringing that much desired EDGE to your business.
