Product Features


An All-in-One Cloud-based solution that manages product distribution from source to consumer. EDGE Pharma ensures efficient product management, visibility, and control all through the supply chain.

Manufacturers
& Importers
  • Product Definition & Identification.
  • Product Batch, LOT and Life Cycle Management.
  • Stock State Management.
  • Product Price Management.
  • Credit Management & Reconciliation.
  • Product Recall Management.
  • Order To Cash (OTC).
Distribution Centers
& Warehouses
  • Order Processing and Tracking.
  • Order Fulfilment (Pickup, Release, and Delivery).
  • Stock Management (Receipts, Issues, Transfers, and Adjustments).
  • Warehouse Storage Management (Space Planning, Product Allocation, Palletization and Racking).
  • Product conversion (Bulk Breaking, Assemblage & Kitting).
Distributors
& Retailers
  • Online Ordering.
  • Inventory Management.
  • Sales Management.
  • Retail Outlet Management.
  • Online Product Visibility for Distributors.
  • Order To Cash (OTC).
  • POS integration for retail.
Consumers
  • Consumer feedback/Interaction.
  • Pharmaco-Vigilance (for Pharmaceuticals).
  • Consumer complaints.
