Product Features


An All-in-One Cloud-based solution that manages product distribution from source to consumer. The solution ensures efficient product management, visibility, and control all through the supply chain.

Manufacturers
& Importers
  • Product Definition & Identification.
  • Product Batch, LOT and Life Cycle Management.
  • Stock State Management.
  • Product Price Management.
  • Credit Management & Reconciliation.
  • Product Recall Management.
  • Order To Cash (OTC).
Distribution Centers
& Warehouses
  • Order Processing and Tracking.
  • Order Fulfilment (Pickup, Release, and Delivery).
  • Stock Management (Receipts, Issues, Transfers, and Adjustments).
  • Warehouse Storage Management (Space planning, Product Allocation, Palletization and Racking).
  • Product conversion (Bulk Breaking, Assemblage & Kitting).
Distributors
& Retailers
  • Online Ordering.
  • Inventory Management.
  • Sales Management.
  • Retail Outlet Management.
  • Online Product Visibility for Distributors.
  • Order To Cash (OTC).
  • POS integration for retail.
Consumers
  • Consumer feedback/Interaction.
  • Pharmaco-Vigilance (for Pharmaceuticals).
  • Consumer complaints.


Our Process

Connecting different participants in the supply chain under a single platform.

01

Manufacturers & Importers

...Goods are brought into EDGE after manufacturing or importation. These goods can then be pushed to various distribution centres / warehouses for onward distribution.

02

Distribution Centers & Warehouses

...Product putaway, Storage, Packaging / Re-packaging, Order processing, Pickup, Release, and Delivery to Distributors and Retailers.

03

Distributors & Retailers

...Online order placement and Payment, Inventory Management, Wholesale and Retail.

04

Consumers

...Complaints, Feedback and Comments on Product Consumption.
